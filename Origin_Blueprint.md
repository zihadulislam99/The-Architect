# Origin Blueprint

## Overview

**Origin Blueprint** is a decentralized, off-grid civilizational knowledge infrastructure designed to preserve, organize, verify, teach, and continuously expand humanity's essential knowledge for future generations, even in scenarios where the internet, cloud infrastructure, power grids, or modern communication networks are unavailable.

The project combines the principles of long-term knowledge preservation with structured education, skill development, and community-driven verification. Rather than functioning solely as a digital archive, Origin Blueprint is designed as a complete knowledge ecosystem capable of transforming information into practical human capability.

The system is intended to operate on low-power hardware such as Raspberry Pi devices, local servers, portable storage systems, and future decentralized networks. It is designed to remain functional independently of centralized infrastructure while maintaining access to the scientific, technical, educational, cultural, and practical knowledge necessary for the survival, rebuilding, and advancement of civilization.

---

# Mission

To preserve humanity's collective knowledge, organize it into accessible learning structures, verify its accuracy, and ensure future generations can acquire the skills necessary to sustain, rebuild, and advance civilization regardless of technological, environmental, economic, or societal disruptions.

# Knowledge Hierarchy Framework

Origin Blueprint organizes knowledge through a structured multi-layer hierarchy designed to support preservation, education, verification, discovery, and practical skill development.

Unlike traditional archives that primarily categorize documents, Origin Blueprint organizes information into interconnected knowledge objects, concepts, skills, competencies, and professional capabilities.

The hierarchy is designed to represent not only what humanity knows, but also how that knowledge can be learned, applied, verified, and transmitted across generations.

## Hierarchical Structure

```text
Civilization
│
├── Domain
│   ├── Discipline
│   │   ├── Subject
│   │   │   ├── Topic
│   │   │   │   ├── Concept
│   │   │   │   ├── Procedure
│   │   │   │   ├── Skill
│   │   │   │   ├── Assessment
│   │   │   │   └── Resource
│   │   │   │
│   │   │   └── Learning Module
│   │   │
│   │   └── Learning Path
│   │
│   └── Competency
│
└── Profession
```

## Civilization Layer

Examples:
